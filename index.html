About

A decade of keeping other people's books honest.

I've spent over nine years inside corporate accounting departments in the Philippines — reconciling bank accounts, processing supplier payments, running payroll, and closing the books every single month without fail. Most recently, I manage purchasing and reconciliation for a grain trading company, work that leaves no room for a number that doesn't add up.

I'm now taking that same discipline into remote work — part-time, evenings, for small businesses in the UK, US, Australia, and beyond who need their books accurate and up to date without hiring a full-time in-house bookkeeper.

Certified in Xero (Advisor, Level 1 & 2), comfortable in QuickBooks Online, and communicate clearly over email and Slack — because a bookkeeper you can't reach is worse than no bookkeeper at all.
